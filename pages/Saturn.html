Saturn

Saturn is famous for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock.
It is also a gas giant and has many moons orbiting around it.
Despite its size, Saturn is very light compared to other planets.

Saturn is famous for its beautiful ring system made of ice and rock particles.
Like Jupiter, it is a gas giant composed mainly of hydrogen and helium.

Saturn is extremely light for its size — it could theoretically float in water if a large enough ocean existed!
It has over 140 moons, including Titan, which has lakes of liquid methane.
Average temperature: about −178°C.
