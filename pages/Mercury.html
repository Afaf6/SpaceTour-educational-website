Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the smallest planet in our solar system.
It has extremely hot days and very cold nights because it has almost no atmosphere.
A year on Mercury lasts only 88 Earth days.

Uranus is an ice giant with a blue-green color caused by methane gas in its atmosphere.
It rotates on its side, making it unique among planets — seasons can last over 20 Earth years.

Temperatures can drop to −224°C, making it one of the coldest planets. Uranus also has faint rings and many icy moons.
