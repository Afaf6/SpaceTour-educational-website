Neptune

Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun.
It is known for powerful winds — the fastest in the solar system.
Its deep blue color makes it one of the most beautiful planets.

Neptune is the farthest known planet from the Sun and is famous for extremely strong winds
the fastest in the solar system, reaching over 2,000 km/h.

Its deep blue color comes from methane gas.
Neptune has a large moon called Triton, which orbits in the opposite direction of the planet’s rotation, suggesting it was captured by gravity long ago.
Average temperature: about −214°C.
