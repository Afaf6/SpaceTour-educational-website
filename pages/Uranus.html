Uranus

Uranus rotates on its side, making it unique among planets.
It has a pale blue color caused by methane gas in its atmosphere.
It is an icy giant with extremely cold temperatures.

Neptune is the farthest known planet from the Sun and is famous for extremely strong winds
the fastest in the solar system, reaching over 2,000 km/h.

Its deep blue color comes from methane gas.
Neptune has a large moon called Triton, which orbits in the opposite direction of the planet’s rotation, suggesting it was captured.
Average temperature: about −214°C.
