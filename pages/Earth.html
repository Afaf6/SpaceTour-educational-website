Earth

Earth is the only known planet that supports life.
It has water, oxygen, and a protective atmosphere.
About 70% of Earth’s surface is covered by oceans.

Earth is the only known planet that supports life. About 71% of its surface is covered with water, which plays a major role in regulating temperature and sustaining ecosystems.
Earth’s atmosphere protects life by blocking harmful radiation and maintaining breathable air.

The average surface temperature is about 15°C, ideal for living organisms.
Earth has one natural satellite — the Moon — which stabilizes the planet’s rotation and affects ocean tides.
