About SpaceTour

SpaceTour is a fictional educational website inspired by NASA missions. It serves as a scientific reference for students who want to review accurate information about planets, astronauts, and space exploration. The platform combines learning with interactive design to make science more engaging and accessible.

Through this website, users can explore planets, learn about famous astronauts, and imagine booking a future journey beyond Earth. SpaceTour combines science, curiosity, and innovation to inspire the next generation of space explorers.
